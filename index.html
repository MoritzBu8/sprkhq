About SprkHQ

SprkHQ is SoCal's ultimate fusion: a vibrant restaurant on one side of a crystal-clear glass wall, paired with a premier car mechanic shop on the other. Gearheads and foodies unite—savor farm-fresh Cali cuisine while watching classic cars get restored, engines roar, and enthusiasts showcase their rides in our sun-drenched lot, backed by palm trees and ocean breezes.

It's the spot where wrench-turning meets plate-spinning, delivering adrenaline-fueled vibes, mouthwatering meals, and community for car lovers. Park your pride, grab a bite, and stay for the show.
